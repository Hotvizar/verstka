Who We are

The company "Avto Delo" has been operatingin the Ukrainian market since 2009

We develop and manufacture training equipment for driving schools, specialized educational institutions, and motor transport enterprises. The use of our products enables us to organize the learning process in the most effective way, making it more comfortable and accessible for students.

At the client's request, our company's specialists can customize the size, quantity, and type of products ordered or create custom goods based on the customer's individual design.

Driving Simulators
educational equipment
LAYOUTS OF TRANSPORT UNITS

Our advantages:

We utilize cutting-edge technologies and materials, combined with the expertise and knowledge of our highly qualified specialists. This enables our company to manufacture high-quality products that adhere to safety and ergonomic standards.

Our products have successfully undergone testing by the state accreditation commission and received approval from all regulatory authorities in Ukraine for use in educational institutions.

Our extensive experience, investments in modern technologies, and innovative production approach drive continuous improvement in product quality, as well as the expansion and updating of our range of goods and services.

We are always prepared to collaborate and tailor an individual approach to each client. We offer a flexible payment system, professional guidance on any inquiries, as well as warranty and post-warranty service.
