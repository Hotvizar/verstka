What is included in the cleanup?

Bath
  • Shine the bathtub/shower cubicle
  • Wash and wipe down all taps and faucets
  • Polish all mirrors and glass surfaces
  • Toilet bowl cleaning and disinfectio
Rooms
  • Make the bed
  • Dust the bed
  • Light fixtures and switches, doorknobs
  • Wipe down surfaces, cupboards
  • Sort a small amount of stuff
  • Polish wooden surfaces
  • Mop and vacuum the floors
Kitchen
  • Wash surfaces and tiles
  • Clean the kitchen set, furniture fronts
  • Clean the outside of appliances
  • Wash the dishes in the sink
  • Take out the garbage
