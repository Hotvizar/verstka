About us

The company "Avto Delo" has been developing and manufacturing modern training equipment for any class of drivers since 2009. The process of learning to drive and improving the skills of drivers will be much more effective if modern training tools are used. Among them are car driving simulators, lab stands, and much more, which will make the learning process more accessible to students. Having our own production base, we control the full production cycle, and our employees will help and advise you on any issues.

Cooperation with us is a step towards to road safety!
